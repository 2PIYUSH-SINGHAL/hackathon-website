THE EVENT
what is this?

A 24-hour online hackathon, organised and run by the students of Welham Boys' School, for schools across the country. Build a working thing in one of three tracks, demo it to a panel of judges, make friends, lose sleep, earn a certificate.

We supply: mentors, workshops, problem statements, a welcoming chat room. You supply: a team, a laptop, and the ideas.

Registration guidelines

One team per school · filled by the school
  1. 01
    Teacher-in-charge forms a team of 2–4 students (classes 9–12).
  2. 02
    One team per school — no school may send more than one entry.
  3. 03
    The school (not students) fills and submits the Google Form.
  4. 04
    Download and read the registration guide (PDF) before filling.
  5. 05
    Await confirmation within three working days.
  6. 06
    Attend the briefing call the week prior.
  7. 07
    Show up on 31 July 2026, 09:00 IST.
